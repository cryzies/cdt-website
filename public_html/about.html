About Us

$HACKING == true && echo 'Veni. Vidi. Vici.' || rm -rf

The SDSU Cyber Defense Team was founded Spring 2015. President and Founder Alex Salerno was voluntold by Cal Poly Pomona Professor, Dr. Dan Manson, and SDSU Professor, Peter Bartoli, to create a Computer Security Team to represent San Diego State University.

Since then we have strived to become a powerhouse in all things computer security. We use techniques such as Social Engineering, Spoofing, Fuzzing, Exploitations, Steganography, Injections, Cryptography, and more to push the limits of a system in order to detect its' weak points so that we can strengthen them.

We train the next generation of SDSU cyber warriors to protect and defend.
